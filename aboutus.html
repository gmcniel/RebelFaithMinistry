About Us

Rebel Faith Ministry is a non-denominational Christian ministry that has Jesus Christ as the head of the organization with Pastor Geoffrey McNiel as the senior pastor. We're a ministry that is for all believers, especially to those that have become an outcast to the Church. Pastor Geoffrey McNiel has his ordination from the Universal Life Church Ministries and not from any seminary and not from any college instituion as it is not scripturally supported.

    Assemblies of God
